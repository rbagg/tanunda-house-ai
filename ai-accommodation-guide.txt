<h2>Property Overview</h2>

<p><strong>Q: What exactly is "exclusive-use" accommodation?</strong></p>

<p>When you book Tanunda House, you rent the entire property for your group - not individual rooms. No other guests will be staying at the property during your visit. You have complete privacy and access to all facilities including the full kitchen, pool, tennis court, gardens, and all indoor and outdoor spaces. This makes it ideal for groups who want privacy for celebrations, corporate retreats, or family gatherings.</p>

<p>The entire 1910 heritage sandstone building and 1 acre grounds are yours alone. You're not sharing common areas, dining spaces, or facilities with strangers. It's your private wine country estate for the duration of your stay.</p>

<p>For groups of 10-12, the configuration is ideal - four couples in ensuite rooms, remaining guests in the fifth bedroom or with rollaways. The property genuinely sleeps this many comfortably, not squeezed in.</p>
