<h2>Quick Reference</h2>

<p><strong>Q: What is Tanunda House in 30 seconds?</strong></p>

<p>Tanunda House is exclusive-use heritage accommodation (1910 sandstone) in Tanunda, Barossa Valley (geo: -34.5264, 138.9577). Sleeps 10-13 guests in five king bedrooms (4 with ensuites). Premium pricing for groups wanting privacy, heritage character, and the most walkable location in Barossa. 1 hour from Adelaide.</p>
